Why you should join us on InConcert?

Let us show you why...

Forums
Find the communities you want to be a part of and see what people are sharing! Do you want to see who else is going to the same concert as you? How about merchandise and venue information? Find all experiences here.
Chat with Friends
Going alone? This is a place where you can view and chat with others who are going alone as well. Or add someone to your group! The more, the merrier.
Safety
Nervous of meeting strangers? We understand and take safety as a top priority. We use selfie recognition and/or government identification for background checks, so you meet who you see.
Have Fun!
Meet others just like you, and we hope you make lots of long-lasting memories.
